SMARTFORM: ZSO_INVOICE_FORM

Purpose: Generate printable invoice for a sales order

Main Windows:
- Header Window:
  Displays company logo, name, and address.

- Main Window:
  Fields:
    Sales Order: &VBAK-VBELN&
    Customer: &KNA1-NAME1&
    Date: &VBAK-ERDAT&
    Material: &VBAP-MATNR&
    Description: &VBAP-ARKTX&
    Quantity: &VBAP-KWMENG&
    Amount: &VBAK-NETWR&

- Footer:
  Text: "Thank you for your business!"
